Frequently Asked Questions

You can check your Apple warranty status online through the official Apple coverage checker. You will need to enter the serial number of your device, which can be found in the Settings app under General > About.

Apple offers several repair options, including mail-in service and carry-in repairs at an Saud store or an Apple Authorized Service Provider. To start a repair request, visit the Apple Support website and select your product.

If you've forgotten your Apple ID password, you can reset it from any trusted device or by visiting iforgot.apple.com. You'll be guided through steps to verify your identity and set a new password.
